PHQ-9 Questionnaire

The PHQ-9 questionnaire will present to you various questions regarding depression severity and will generally take the form of asking if you've been feeeling a specific way recently. You will be presented with various options to answer such as "Several Days" or "Nearly every day", which will correspond to different values. At the end of the questionnaire a score will be given to you, as well as a descriptor of what this score means.

In addition a machine learning model will be utilised in order to predict your emotions from your facial expression. This will be used at the end of the questionnaire to calculate an additional metric which will attempt to quantify your emotional state during the questionnaire. This will range from either positive, neutral, or negative, wherein positive and negtative correspond to positive and negative emotions. For example, happy is a positive emotion whereas anger is definitely negative.
